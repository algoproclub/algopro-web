Our Activities

Competition Preparation Courses

Online C++ courses on weekday afternoons - from introductions to competitive programming to preparation for international contests, for all levels

Introduction to the World of Programming

Beginner Python online course on Tuesday or Friday afternoons - no prerequisites required

International Competitions

Regular participation in international online contests (e.g., Codeforces, AtCoder, CodeChef) together, followed by task discussions

Weekend Programs

Weekend consultations, workshops, and unique special competitions

Community Building

Occasional in-person meetups for community building

Join the club!

You can join our sessions for a monthly membership fee, which covers participation in all our events. Every member chooses their own fee, a sum divisible by 1000 HUF.

To join, just fill out the simple form accessible with the button below, and we'll contact you via email!

Would you like to join the organizing team? In that case, send us an email at info@algopro.hu!
